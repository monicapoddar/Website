My Story

I

am a 24 year old designer from India with a passion towards travel, food, culture, peace, DOTA and minimalism. Currently I am working as a UX Designer at Nutanix, to help build multi cloud architechtures and simple infrastructure management.

“The role of the designer is that of a good, thoughtful host anticipating the needs of his guests.”
– Charles Eames

I strive for achieving progressive user experiences for the imminent user needs, continuously learning to build empathy with people and designing on mediums that help them achieve more. I am fascinated by the interactions between people and the role played by design in the symbiosis of humans and digital products.
