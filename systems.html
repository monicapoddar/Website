Assignments

Week 1 : Visual Systems | Playing Cards
WEEK 1 : VISUAL SYSTEMS | PLAYING CARDS
Identifying major elements and rules of a visual system followed by adding new components to the system.
Week 2 : Counting Systems
WEEK 2 : COUNTING SYSTEMS
Animating a 7 segment LCD digital numeric display in base 2 and further exploring the animation of a 6 segment display in base 3.
Week 3 : Communication Systems
WEEK 3 : COMMUNICATION SYSTEMS
Creating a new code to transmit messages across a distance.
Week 4 : Application Data Model
WEEK 4 : APPLICATION DATA MODEL
Understanding the workings of a desktop application.
Week 5 : Poetic forms | Quatrain
WEEK 5 : POETIC FORMS | QUATRAIN
A visual diagram of the structure of a quatrain and an interactive game to create your own.
Week 6 : Design Systems
WEEK 6 : DESIGN SYSTEMS
Analyzing Massimo Vignelli’s design for the original Audubon Field Guides.
Week 7: Interactive Models | My Family Tree
WEEK 7: INTERACTIVE MODELS | MY FAMILY TREE
Creating an interactive model of my family tree in timeline, list and tree views.
