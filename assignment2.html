WEEK 2
COUNTING SYSTEMS

Animating a 7 segment LCD digital numeric display in base 2 and further exploring the animation of a 6 segment display in base 3.

The first animation shows every possible combination of on and off where 0 = off and 1 = on for a 7 segment LCD digital numeric display. Each combination is represented by a base 2 number, i.e., 000 0000 to 111 1111. The second animation displays the segmented numerals from 0 to 9.

I further tried exploring how base 3 numbers can be displayed in case of 6 segments, i.e. 000000... 111111,.... 222222. The third animation shows every possible combination of varied opacity where 0 = 0% opacity, 1 = 50% opacity and 2 = 100% opacity. The fourth animation displays the segmented numerals from 0 to 9 with both 50% and 100% opacity.
