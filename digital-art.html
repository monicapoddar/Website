A collection of digital product renders, and illustrations.

"Cultural myth and lore honor the butterfly as a symbol of transformation because of its impressive process of metamorphosis."

Wings is an abstract illustration to capture the social and political chains that surround people not letting them break free. The butterfly wing is rooted to its surroundings, unable to move as time ticks by and its life decreases. The caption suggests that its never too late for change, even if you have little time you can still do a lot only if you want.
